Data Science Department

The Department of Data Science is dedicated to the study and application of data-driven methodologies to extract meaningful insights and drive decision-making across various domains. With the increasing availability of vast amounts of data, the department focuses on equipping students with a deep understanding of statistical analysis, machine learning, data mining, data visualization, and big data technologies. Students are trained to analyze complex datasets, build predictive models, and communicate results effectively to support informed decision-making in industries such as healthcare, finance, marketing, and technology.
